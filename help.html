Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1) What is the AYUSH Startup Portal?
The AYUSH Startup Portal is an online platform developed by the Ministry of AYUSH to streamline the registration and application processes for startups in the AYUSH sector. It also enables startups to apply for various schemes and track their applications in real-time.
2) Who can register on the AYUSH Startup Portal?
Any startup that operates in the AYUSH sector, including Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy, is eligible to register on the portal. Specific eligibility criteria may apply depending on the scheme.
3) How do I create an account on the AYUSH Startup Portal?
To create an account, click on the "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your personal and business details, upload the required documents, and set a password. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
4) What documents are required for registration?
Documents such as proof of identity, business registration certificates, and relevant project details may be required. Specific document requirements will vary depending on the schemes you are applying for.
5) How can I track the status of my application?
After submitting your application, you can monitor its status by logging into your account. Go to the "My Applications" section to view updates on your application's progress in real-time.
6) How long does it take to process my application?
The time taken to process applications may vary depending on the scheme and the completeness of your application. Once submitted, you will be notified of the expected processing time.
7) Can I edit my application after submission?
No, once your application is submitted, you cannot make changes. However, if you need to update your information or documents, contact support for assistance.
8) What if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you will receive a link to reset your password.
9) Is my data safe on the AYUSH Startup Portal?
Yes, the portal uses secure encryption methods to protect your personal and business data. The Ministry of AYUSH is committed to ensuring the confidentiality and privacy of all users.
10) What schemes are available on the AYUSH Startup Portal?
The portal hosts various schemes, including:
  • National AYUSH Mission (NAM)
  • AYUSH Entrepreneur Development Program (AEDP)
  • AYUSH Research and Development
  • AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (H&WC)
More details are available in the "Schemes" section.
11) How do I contact support if I face any issues?
You can contact the AYUSH support team by using the contact form on the portal or calling the support helpline during business hours. Support details are provided on the Contact page.
12) Is there a fee to register or apply through the portal?
No, there is no fee for registering or applying for schemes through the AYUSH Startup Portal. It is free for all eligible startups.
13) What are the benefits of using the AYUSH Startup Portal?
The portal provides a centralized platform for startups to register, apply for government schemes, and track their applications. It ensures transparency, efficiency, and faster processing of applications.
